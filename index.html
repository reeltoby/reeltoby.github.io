Toby's Coffee Shop

At Toby's Coffee shop, we provide different flavours from all corners of the world, giving you a unique coffee experience. The word "Coffee" got into the English Language via the Dutch word "Koffie", borrowed from Ottoman Turkish "kahve", borrowed in turn from the Arabic word "qahwah". Drinking coffee is beneficial to healthy adults as it acts as stimulants. There are ongoing research on whether long term consumption of coffee reduces the risk of some diseases.
